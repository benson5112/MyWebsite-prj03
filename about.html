About Fran

The Story Behind Fran's Flowers

Meet Francine Miller

From Passion to Profession

Francine "Fran" Miller has always believed that flowers have the power to change moments into memories. Growing up in San Francisco, she spent her childhood in her grandmother's garden, learning the names of every flower and discovering their unique beauty. What started as a childhood hobby blossomed into a lifelong passion.

Founding Fran's Flowers

In 1995, Fran opened the doors of her small floral shop in downtown San Francisco with a simple mission: to create the most beautiful, fresh, and meaningful flower arrangements in the city. She believed that every occasion—big or small—deserved special attention, and that flowers should tell a story.

A Commitment to Quality

For nearly 30 years, Fran has maintained her commitment to sourcing the freshest flowers, whether from local growers or trusted suppliers around the world. She spends hours each morning selecting and arranging flowers by hand, ensuring that every bouquet that leaves her shop is perfect. Her attention to detail and genuine care for her customers have made Fran's Flowers a beloved fixture in San Francisco.

Our Philosophy

Fran believes that flowers aren't just a nice gift—they're a way to express feelings that sometimes words can't capture. Whether it's a dozen red roses for an anniversary, a cheerful sunflower arrangement for a friend, or an elegant wedding installation, Fran approaches each arrangement with the same level of care and creativity. She often says, "Every flower has a purpose, and every arrangement has a story."

Beyond the Shop

When she's not designing arrangements, Fran loves to teach floral design workshops for the community, giving back to San Francisco by sharing her passion for flowers. She's also an avid gardener, and her own rooftop garden provides inspiration for seasonal arrangements that keep her work fresh and innovative.
